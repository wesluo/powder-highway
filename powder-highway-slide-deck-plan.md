## Visual Style Guidelines

### Colors
- Primary: Deep blue (#0D47A1) for Epic
- Secondary: Red (#C62828) for IKON
- Accent: Gold (#FFB300) for Powder Zone
- Background: Dark grays and whites
- Text: High contrast white on dark, dark on light

### Typography
- Headers: Bold sans-serif (Bebas Neue, Anton, or similar)
- Body: Clean readable sans-serif (Open Sans, Roboto)
- Numbers/Stats: Large, bold, high impact

### Image Style
- Hero images: Full-bleed powder shots
- Maps: Clean, minimalist with clear route lines
- Icons: Simple, recognizable resort/mountain symbols
- Photos: Action shots over scenic when possible

### Layout Principles
- Consistent header placement
- Clear visual hierarchy
- White space for breathing room
- Mobile-friendly aspect ratio considerations
